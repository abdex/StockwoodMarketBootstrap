Welcome to Stockwood Market

The Stockwood Market is a local street market, supporting the local community and local businesses. It is 10am – 2pm on the last Saturday of the month from April to November.

Follow us on Facebook for the latest news on stalls and other activities.

The market has a variety of food, including hot food, cold storage food, ice cream and multiple cake stalls (with gluten free options available).

Our local traders sell a wide range of crafts, jewellery, woodwork, and more.

Several local organisations have stalls to help promote the amazing work they do in the community.

We have a variety of entertainment at every market. Our DJ plays music to keep us dancing, and we often have local performers such as Zumba and the church choir. We also have activities for children, including Lego, games and seasonal crafts.

Support the Stockwood Market We need your help.

There are lots of ways you can support the market, as well as joining us on the day and being involved in the vibrant community.

Volunteering on the Day

We need volunteers to help us set up and take down the market! This includes loading the equipment and setting up 30-40 tables and parasols, and then taking them down again. We have a great team who help but we are always looking for more volunteers to help make the process as quick and smooth as possible. If you could spare some time, either 7am – 9am in the morning, or 2pm – 4pm in the afternoon, please reach out to the market team via Facebook or our email stockwoodmarket@gmail.com.

Donate

The market is a not-for-profit self-funded organisation. We use the income from the stall owners to pay for licences, entertainment and other running costs. If you would like to donate, to help keep the market running, please click the link here.
You can also donate your spare change at the markets, look out for the two donations boxes.

Sponsor

If you would like to sponsor the market, with a monthly donation, you will be promoted through our website, Facebook posts and at the markets with signs.
The market is a not-for-profit self-funded organisation, and we rely on donations and sponsorship to help us afford the additional entertainment provided, such as our amazing DJ and children’s activities.
If you would like to become a sponsor of the Stockwood Market, please reach out to the market team via Facebook or our email stockwoodmarket@gmail.com.
